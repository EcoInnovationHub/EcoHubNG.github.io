About
Eco-Hub

Eco-Innovation Hub (Eco-Hub) is an ecosystem of entrepreneurs from various fields of specialization. Started up in 2013 and has been able to spring up through the hard times of gathering her members to developing to fulfilling her vision. The hub is located at No. 3 Olukayode house, Alagbaka, Akure.

Over the period of operation, the hub has been able to organize different tech events and executed a couple of projects in the area.

Eco-innovation Hub is a synergy of young, passionate, enterprising, non-convectional, enthusiastic and result oriented individuals who share an interest of making a change and impacting lives.

With the drive for the need to create an enabling environment for 'Techy and Smart individuals' led by Mr. Constant Asabia (Co-Founder Eco Hub Innovation Akure) and was supported by group of young intellectuals mostly undergraduates from Federal university of Technology, Akure.
